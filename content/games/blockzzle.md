Blockzzle is a free block puzzle game online. You can play instantly in the browser, place block pieces on the board, clear lines, and chase your best score without installing an app.

[Play Blockzzle](https://blockzzle.com/play/)

## No-Install Browser Play

Blockzzle is built for quick web play. There is no install step, no login requirement, and no email address needed before you start playing.

## How The Block Puzzle Works

The core loop is about placing block pieces carefully, making room for the next shapes, and clearing full lines. Each line clear opens space on the board, and stronger placement choices help you keep a run alive while you chase a better score.

## Leaderboard

Blockzzle supports an anonymous leaderboard, so players can compare scores without creating an account or sharing an email address.
