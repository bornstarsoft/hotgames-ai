## What Key9 Means

Key9 is the first step in the puzzle flow: find the Key9 first, then continue to the full Sudoku board.

## Who It Is For

DaySudoku is for players who want a short daily logic puzzle in the browser. It sits alongside HotGames.ai puzzle picks as a Sudoku game, separate from Blockzzle's block pieces and Ringzzle's color rings.
