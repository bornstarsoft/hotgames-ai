# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2025-12-17

HotGames.ai (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect and how we use it when you visit **hotgames.ai**.

## 1) What we collect

### A. Analytics data (GA4)
We use **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** to understand traffic and improve the site. This may include:
- pages you visit and basic interaction events
- approximate location (city-level)
- device / browser information
- referral sources (where you came from)

### B. Cookies and similar technologies
Analytics tools may use cookies or similar technologies to measure usage and improve the website.

### C. Contact messages
If you email us, we will receive the information you include (such as your email address and the contents of your message).

## 2) How we use information
We use the information to:
- operate, maintain, and improve HotGames.ai
- understand which pages and game links are helpful
- detect abuse, prevent fraud, and maintain security
- respond to inquiries and support requests

## 3) Legal bases (where applicable)
Depending on your location, we may process data based on:
- our legitimate interests (analytics, security, site improvement)
- your consent (where required for analytics cookies)
- compliance with legal obligations

## 4) Sharing
We do **not** sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with service providers (for example, analytics vendors such as Google) to operate and improve the website.

## 5) International transfers
Service providers may process data on servers located outside your country. When this happens, we take reasonable steps to use reputable providers and safeguard data as required by applicable law.

## 6) Data retention
- Analytics data is retained according to our analytics provider settings and policies.
- Email inquiries are retained as long as reasonably necessary to respond and maintain business records, then deleted or anonymized where feasible.

## 7) Your choices
You can:
- block or delete cookies in your browser settings
- use browser settings or extensions to limit tracking
- use Google’s tools (where available) to manage Google Analytics/ads preferences

## 8) Third-party links
HotGames.ai contains links to third-party websites (game stores, news sources, etc.). Their privacy practices may differ. Please review their policies before using their services.

## 9) Children’s privacy
HotGames.ai is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10) Security
We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. However, no website can guarantee absolute security.

## 11) Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
