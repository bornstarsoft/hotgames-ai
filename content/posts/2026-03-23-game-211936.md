KONAMI is set to delight fans with the upcoming release of **Picross S: Konami Antiques Collection** on April 30, 2023. This new addition to the beloved Picross series will feature 585 puzzles inspired by over 80 classic Konami games, including iconic titles like *Castlevania*, *Metal Gear*, and *Contra*. Players will engage in the familiar grid-based puzzle-solving that reveals pixel art sprites from their favorite retro games, making it a nostalgic experience for gamers of all ages.

Developed by Jupiter, this game promises a rich variety of challenges, including 150 standard Picross puzzles, 150 Mega Picross puzzles, and even a Time Attack mode. With support for mouse controls and touchscreen functionality on the Nintendo Switch, it caters to both traditional and modern gaming preferences. As the ninth installment in the Picross S series, it joins a lineup that has already captivated audiences with themes from Sega, Capcom, and more.

As we look forward to the release, one can't help but wonder how this game will resonate with both longtime fans of Konami and newcomers to the Picross genre. Will it reignite the passion for classic gaming, or will it pave the way for new fans to discover the rich history of Konami's game library?
