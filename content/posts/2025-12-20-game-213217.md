Despite facing significant online scrutiny and controversy, this latest installment in the beloved franchise has captivated a substantial audience, proving that strong narratives and immersive gameplay can still resonate with gamers.

According to a recent report by industry analysts at Newzoo, Assassins Creed Shadows has managed to secure a prominent position on the sales charts, even amidst fierce competition from established franchises like Electronic Arts' sports titles and the Battlefield series. This success highlights the resilience of the Assassins Creed brand and its ability to thrive despite mixed reviews.
