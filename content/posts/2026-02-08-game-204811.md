If you're a fan of puzzle games but often find them too challenging, then Botany Manor might just be the perfect fit for you. Currently available for free on the Epic Games Store, this cozy first-person puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of botanist Arabella Greene as she explores a Victorian manor to complete her botanical research. Unlike other puzzle games that can leave you feeling frustrated, Botany Manor strikes a balance between engaging challenges and a relaxing atmosphere.

The developers have crafted a game that encourages exploration without overwhelming players with complexity. Lead developer Laure De May emphasizes that the puzzles are designed to be thoughtful yet accessible, making it ideal for casual gamers. With a peaceful setting and a focus on discovery, Botany Manor offers a refreshing alternative to more intense puzzle experiences like The Witness or Blue Prince.

As you dive into this enchanting world, you'll find that Botany Manor not only provides a delightful gaming experience but also serves as a reminder that gaming can be both fun and relaxing. Have you tried it yet, or are you ready to take a break from the high-stakes puzzle games?
