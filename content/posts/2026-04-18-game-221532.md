PEARL ABYSS has made headlines with its open-world action-adventure title, 'Crimson Desert', surpassing 5 million copies in global sales within just 26 days of its launch. This remarkable achievement marks a significant milestone in Korean console gaming history, showcasing the game's immense popularity and the growing influence of Korean developers in the global gaming market.

The company announced this milestone on April 15, expressing gratitude to the community of players known as Greymanes who have engaged with the game and contributed to its success. Notably, 'Crimson Desert' achieved this sales figure faster than any other Korean console game, outpacing previous records, including the acclaimed 'Clair Obscur: Expedition 33', which took five months to reach the same sales figure.
