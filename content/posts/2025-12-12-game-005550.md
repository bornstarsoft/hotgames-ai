Jonathon Blow, the visionary behind the acclaimed puzzle games Braid and The Witness, is set to unveil his next project at The Game Awards. In a recent tweet, he hinted at an exciting announcement that fans have been eagerly anticipating. The event kicks off at 4:30 PM Pacific / 7:30 PM Eastern, and it promises to be a thrilling moment for gamers and developers alike.

The upcoming title, reportedly named Order of the Sinking Star, is described as a Sokoban-style puzzle game. Blow has been sharing glimpses of its development online, allowing fans to get a sneak peek before the official reveal. This approach not only builds anticipation but also engages the community in the creative process, showcasing a modern trend in game development.

As we await the official announcement, one can't help but wonder how this new game will push the boundaries of puzzle design and storytelling, much like its predecessors. What innovative mechanics and narratives will Blow bring to the table this time?
