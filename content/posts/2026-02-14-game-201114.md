The 2026 Taipei International Game Show showcased the vibrant world of gaming, and the "Dolls Frontline 2" booth was a standout attraction. With a plethora of talented cosplayers bringing beloved characters to life, the booth not only celebrated the game but also created an engaging experience for fans. This event highlighted how cosplay can enhance the gaming experience, fostering a community where fans can connect and share their passion.
