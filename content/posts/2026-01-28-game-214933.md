The gaming world is buzzing with the recent launch of "Omi Oh My AI," a unique ARG-lite puzzle game that invites players to explore the fascinating relationship between humans and artificial intelligence. Developed by EverySecond Studio, this game features over 300 puzzles designed by the quirky AI, Omi, and encourages players to think critically about AI's role in our lives.

Set against a backdrop inspired by the 90s and the classic game "I Love Bees," Omi Oh My AI offers a refreshing take on puzzle-solving. Players can expect to engage with a variety of challenges, from image and word puzzles to number games, all while enjoying a narrative that prompts reflection on our interactions with technology. With a launch price of AU$14.50/US$9.99 and a 20% discount available, its an enticing opportunity for puzzle enthusiasts and AI aficionados alike.

As we delve deeper into the age of AI, games like Omi Oh My AI not only entertain but also educate us about the complexities of our relationship with technology. Will you take on the challenge and see the world through Omi's eyes?
