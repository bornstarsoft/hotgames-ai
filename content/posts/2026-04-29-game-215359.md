In an exciting development for retro gaming enthusiasts, Namco's 1994 arcade game "Cyber Commando" is making its debut on home consoles for the very first time. This classic title, which was previously exclusive to Japanese arcades, is being released by Hamster Corporation as part of their Arcade Archives series. Fans of the original arcade experience can now enjoy this competitive vehicle shooter on platforms like the Switch, PS5, and Xbox Series X/S starting April 30.

"Cyber Commando" is the sequel to the 1993 game "Cyber Sled," and it retains the thrilling gameplay that defined its predecessor. Players will control tanks in a futuristic city on Uranus, battling against a malfunctioning security computer in a space colony. With various strategies unique to each vehicle, the game promises to deliver an exhilarating one-on-one combat experience, enhanced by features like Time Attack Mode and Network Mode for online play.
