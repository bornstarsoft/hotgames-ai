CD Projekt has officially unveiled the highly anticipated third expansion for The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, titled "Songs of the Past," set to launch in 2027. This new chapter will bring players back into the shoes of Geralt of Rivia, the legendary monster slayer, promising a fresh adventure for fans of the franchise. Developed in collaboration with Fools Theory, a team of industry veterans, this expansion is sure to elevate the gaming experience further.

While details remain scarce until a more comprehensive reveal in late summer 2026, the announcement has already stirred excitement among the community. Interestingly, the reveal was initially planned for a live stream, but a premature leak prompted CD Projekt to share the news sooner than expected. With multiple Witcher projects in the pipeline, including The Witcher 4 and a remake of the original game, fans have much to look forward to in the coming years.

As we await more information about "Songs of the Past," one can't help but wonder how this expansion will expand the lore of The Witcher universe and what new challenges await Geralt. Will it live up to the legacy of its predecessors? Only time will tell.
