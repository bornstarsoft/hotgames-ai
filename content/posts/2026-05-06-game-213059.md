In a recent interview, Alix Wilton Regan, the new voice of Lara Croft in the upcoming Tomb Raider games, emphasized the importance of recognizing video game actors for their contributions. Regan pointed out that the work of these performers is often demanding and filled with emotional depth, yet they frequently go unacknowledged compared to their film and television counterparts. 

Regan praised the cast of Baldurs Gate 3 for inspiring a newfound appreciation among video game actors, stating that their performances have helped elevate the entire industry. This recognition is crucial not only for the actors but also for the gaming community, which benefits from the depth and authenticity these performers bring to their roles. 

As the gaming industry continues to evolve, the call for more recognition of performance capture artists and voice actors grows louder. Will we see more awards and categories dedicated to these talented individuals in the future? The conversation is just beginning, and its one worth following closely.
