Get ready for an exhilarating ride as the urban survival game "Good Luck" is set to launch on April 8, 2026, for PC, with later releases for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer challenges players to navigate a chaotic city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards, all while racing to reach a meeting. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, the game promises a unique blend of humor and high-stakes gameplay.

"Good Luck" stands out for its unpredictable physics and lack of checkpoints, making every playthrough a test of skill and patience. Players will face a variety of challenges, from deadly banana peels to treacherous streetlights, ensuring that no two sessions are the same. The game's creators emphasize the chaotic fun of co-op play, where teamwork and strategy are essential to survive the urban jungle.

As we approach the release date, anticipation is building for what promises to be a thrilling addition to the gaming landscape. Will you be ready to tackle the madness of "Good Luck" when it drops?
