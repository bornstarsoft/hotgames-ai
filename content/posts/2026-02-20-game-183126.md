Kodansha Game Lab and Octorain have just unveiled TrailRail, an innovative 2D action puzzle game that promises to captivate players with its unique mechanics and engaging narrative. Set to launch on PC via Steam, the game features a demo available during Steam Next Fest, allowing players to dive into its intriguing world and experience its gameplay firsthand.

In TrailRail, players control Rail, a girl on a quest to uncover her lost memories while navigating through a series of 38 challenging puzzles. The games mechanics revolve around the concept of materializing your movement trail, which can be transformed into various shapes to solve puzzles. This blend of simple controls and complex problem-solving makes for a refreshing gaming experience that encourages creativity and intuition.

As you guide Rail through her journey, you'll encounter a charming robot companion that adds depth to the story. With its ability to express emotions and assist in puzzle-solving, the robot enriches the gameplay and narrative. Will you help Rail uncover her origins and the mysteries of her world? The adventure awaits!
