The Octopath Traveler series has reached an impressive milestone, surpassing 6 million units sold across its titles. This achievement reflects the growing popularity of the franchise, which includes the original Octopath Traveler, its sequel, and the newly released Octopath Traveler 0. The surge in sales comes just a year after the series celebrated 5 million units sold, showcasing a strong upward trend in player engagement and interest.

The first game in the series has been a standout success, with over 3 million units sold by September 2022, while Octopath Traveler II made significant strides, reaching 1 million sales by June 2023. To celebrate this achievement, Square Enix has announced a 60% discount on the first two games, making it an excellent time for new players to dive into this captivating world. As the gaming landscape continues to evolve, one can't help but wonder what the future holds for the Octopath Traveler series and its dedicated fanbase.
