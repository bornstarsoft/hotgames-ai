In an exciting announcement, Jonathan Blow, the visionary behind the acclaimed puzzle games Braid and The Witness, is set to unveil his next project at The Game Awards. This highly anticipated reveal promises to showcase a new title called Order of the Sinking Star, which has been hinted to be a Sokoban-style puzzle game. Fans of Blow's previous works are eager to see how this new game will push the boundaries of puzzle design and storytelling.

Blow has been actively sharing insights into the game's development through social media, building anticipation among his followers. The Game Awards, starting at 4:30 PM Pacific, will be the perfect stage for this reveal, and its clear that the gaming community is buzzing with excitement. As we await further details, one cant help but wonder how Order of the Sinking Star will redefine the puzzle genre and what innovative mechanics Blow has in store for players.
