The release of iRacing's NASCAR 25 Console game marks a significant moment for NASCAR gaming enthusiasts, particularly with its extensive selection of base paint schemes. This game not only brings back fan-favorite designs but also introduces new ones from the ARCA Menards Series, enhancing the overall gaming experience. With the inclusion of iconic sponsors like Red Bull, which hasn't been featured in a NASCAR game since 2011, players are treated to a nostalgic yet fresh visual experience.

While some notable paint schemes and sponsors are absent, such as those from Monster Energy and certain alcoholic brands, the game still manages to captivate fans with its diverse offerings. The variety of base paint schemes available allows players to connect with their favorite drivers and teams in a way that previous titles have not.
