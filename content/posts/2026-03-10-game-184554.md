If you're a fan of cozy games and cats, then the upcoming release of Kitty Haven is sure to pique your interest. This delightful puzzle game invites players to create the perfect sanctuary for their feline friends, each with their own unique quirks and preferences. From greedy eaters to sensitive noses, you'll need to arrange the space thoughtfully to ensure peace and happiness among the kittens.

Kitty Haven not only challenges your problem-solving skills but also allows you to express your creativity by unlocking various items and new residents. The demo is currently available on Steam, giving players a taste of the charming gameplay that awaits. As we look forward to its full release, one can't help but wonder how this game will redefine the cozy gaming genre. Will you be ready to create your own kitty paradise?
