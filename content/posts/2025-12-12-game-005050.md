This December, PlayStation Plus subscribers have a lot to look forward to with the addition of exciting titles like **Assassins Creed Mirage** and **Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty** to the Game Catalog. These games are available for both Extra and Premium tier members, ensuring that players can dive into new adventures just in time for the holiday season.

In addition to these major releases, the catalog also features **Skate Story**, which is already available, along with a variety of family-friendly titles such as **Paw Patrol** games and **Lego Horizon Adventures**. Notably, the classic **Soulcalibur III** joins the Premium Classic Catalog, giving long-time fans a chance to relive a beloved fighting game from the PS2 era.

With such a diverse lineup, December is shaping up to be a fantastic month for gamers on PlayStation Plus. Are you excited to explore these new titles, or will you be revisiting some classic favorites?
