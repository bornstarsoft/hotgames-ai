The Game Awards 2025 delivered a spectacular showcase of over 50 game reveals and announcements, with Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 setting a new record for the most awards won. Among the highlights, the announcement of Star Wars: Fate of the Old Republic, directed by Casey Hudson, reignited excitement for the beloved franchise, while the reveal of a new Divinity game from Larian Studios has fans buzzing about what's next.

Other notable mentions include the debut of Coven of the Chicken Foot, directed by Bruce Straley, and the return of iconic characters in Resident Evil Requiem.
