In a world where technology and emotions intertwine, "Save My Scrap" emerges as a compelling visual novel and puzzle game that invites players to delve into the intricate relationship between a mechanic and a broken android. Set to launch on Steam on April 22, 2026, followed by releases on Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 5, this game promises not only engaging gameplay but also a heart-wrenching narrative that explores themes of connection and memory.

Originally based on the popular free game "Fix My Junk," this upgraded version by AMATA Games introduces new dialogue, endings, and a gallery mode, enhancing the player experience significantly. As you repair the android Harima, you will uncover layers of emotion and forgotten memories, making each interaction meaningful. The games multilingual support ensures that players from various backgrounds can share in this touching story.

With a launch discount available for a limited time, now is the perfect opportunity to experience this unique blend of mechanics and storytelling. As we prepare for the release, one can't help but wonder: how will this game redefine our understanding of connection in an increasingly digital world?
