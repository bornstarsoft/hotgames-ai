Exciting news for gamers! The beloved 3D puzzle game, Fruit Mountain Party, is set to launch on the Nintendo Switch and Switch 2 in spring 2026. This announcement comes on the heels of its successful Early Access release on PC, where players have already been enjoying its unique blend of multiplayer fun and fruit-themed challenges.

Fruit Mountain Party offers a fresh take on puzzle gaming, allowing players to combine fruits to create larger ones while competing in various modes. With features like heated online battles and a single-player mode that supports global rankings, it promises to deliver hours of entertainment for both casual and competitive gamers alike. The game will be showcased at major events, including Osaka Game Dungeon and Tokyo eSports Festa, giving fans a sneak peek before its official release.

As we look forward to its launch, one can't help but wonder how this vibrant game will evolve on the Switch platform. Will it capture the hearts of a new audience? Only time will tell!
