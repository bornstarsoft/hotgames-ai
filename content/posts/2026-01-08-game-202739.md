The recently launched puzzle game AiliA is captivating players with its innovative mirror-based gameplay mechanics. Developed by IceToad Studio LLC and published by Gamirror Games, AiliA invites players into a visually stunning world where reality and reflection intertwine. Priced at $11.99 with a 20% discount during its launch period, this game is a must-try for puzzle enthusiasts looking for a fresh challenge.

In AiliA, players embody a young girl named Aria, navigating through intricately designed stages that span both the real and mirrored worlds. The interactive mirrors are not just for show; they serve as gateways to new paths and puzzle solutions, enhancing the gameplay experience. With over 100 puzzles spread across seven unique themed areas, including boss encounters that cleverly incorporate mirror mechanics, AiliA promises hours of engaging gameplay.

For those curious about the game, a free demo is available on Steam, allowing players to dive into the enchanting world of AiliA and uncover the story of sisters Aria and Lia. Will you be able to solve the mysteries hidden within this reflective realm?
