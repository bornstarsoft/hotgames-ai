In an exciting announcement, Jonathan Blow, the mastermind behind the critically acclaimed puzzle games Braid and The Witness, is set to unveil his next project at The Game Awards. This much-anticipated reveal promises to showcase a fresh take on puzzle mechanics, with Blow hinting at a game titled "Order of the Sinking Star." 

Fans can expect a Sokoban-style puzzle experience, a genre that challenges players to push boxes around a grid to reach designated locations. Blow has been sharing glimpses of the game's development on social media, building anticipation for what could be another groundbreaking title from Thekla. With the reveal just around the corner, the gaming community is buzzing with speculation and excitement. 

As we await further details, one has to wonder: will this new game redefine puzzle gaming as we know it?
